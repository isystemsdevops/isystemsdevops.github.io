Privacy policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this privacy policy. Collection of personal information

We do not receive or store any users account information. The only information that we need to collect from the device is the Geolocation data (latitude and longitude) on main two screens:

  • Homepage when accessing the app to determine the city and country the user is located in to allocate the appropriate prayer times and showing the praying notifications at the exact time the school bus.
  • Qibla page - determine the user location to show the exact Qibla direction.

all other pages in the App does not collect Geolocation data.

You do not need to provide us with any Personal Information, for now the App does not required the creation of personal account, and all app features will be available to you without any restrictions.

Privacy of children

We do not knowingly collect any Personal Information from children under the age of 18 - In the App in general there is no place or fields to add or submit any Personal Information, and all required profile information will be collected at the time of Account creation by the School administration in the presence of the Parents.

If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 18 has provided Personal Information to us through the Mobile Application and Services, please contact us to request that we delete that child’s Personal Information from our Services.

Managing information

The Iqraa app does not require a personal account for now, so no personal information are collected, stored or managed.

Disclosure of information

We will not be sharing any personally identifiable information with third parties and will not share any information with unaffiliated third parties.

Retention of information

We do not need to retain any user data since no personal information will be requested and collected.

Geolocation Information

The app does not collect your location continuously or without your knowledge - it is used only on the Map page where parents can share their home location manually.

The shared location by the user will be used by the school administration to know where the students are distributed in the city and how they can be collected and dropped off from and to their homes in the best way possible.

we don't collect the location history in our Database; only the last shared location will be used.

We use the information obtained from these analytics tools to monitor the performance and improve our Mobile Application and Services. We do not use third-party analytics tools to track or to collect any personally identifiable information of our Users and we will not associate any information gathered from the statistical reports with any individual User.
