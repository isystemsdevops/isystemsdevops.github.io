Overview

This Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide ("Personal Information") in the "White-Cargo" mobile application, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that Personal Information.

It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your Personal Information and how you can access and update it.

Collection of Personal Information

We receive and store all users account information and will be provided to the users, White-Cargo company clients, in the company before using the App. We do not collect your geolocation data on any page inside the App.

You do not need to provide us with any Personal Information. Your username and password will be provided by your organization, and all app features will be available to you without any restrictions. If you do not have an account yet, please contact your HR or IT admin to set up a new account.

Privacy of Children

We do not knowingly collect any Personal Information from children under the age of 18. In the App in general there is no place or fields to add or submit any Personal Information, and all required profile information will be collected at the time of account creation by White-Cargo company administration in the presence of the client.

If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 18 has provided Personal Information to us through the Mobile Application and Services, please contact us to request that we delete that child's Personal Information from our Services.

Use and Processing of Collected Information

In order to make the Mobile Application and Services available to you, or to meet a legal obligation, we may need to collect and use certain Personal Information that is filled during the account creation process by the company administration in the presence of the client.

Some analytics information will be collected anonymously while you are using the App functionalities and navigating the different pages.

Any of the information we collect from you may be used for the following purposes:

  • Improve user experience.
  • Run and operate the Mobile Application core functions.
  • Detect any errors that may occur within the App, which will be investigated and resolved in future App updates.

Processing your Personal Information depends on how you interact with the Mobile Application and Services, where you are located in the world, and if one of the following applies:

  • You have given your consent for one or more specific purposes.
  • Provision of information is necessary for the performance of an agreement with you and/or for any pre-contractual obligations thereof.
  • Processing is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which you are subject.
  • Processing is related to a task that is carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority vested in us.
  • Processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by us or by a third party.

Managing Information

You are not able to delete certain Personal Information through the App. All information is created, added, and managed by White-Cargo company administration.

This information is kept safely and not shared with any other employees, any other clients, or any third-party organization. It is used only for the purpose of managing the systems and user account separately.

If you would like to edit/delete your Personal Information or permanently delete your account, you can simply do so by contacting the company administration or by contacting us.

Disclosure of Information

We will not be sharing any personally identifiable information with third parties and will not share any information with unaffiliated third parties.

Retention of Information

We will retain and use your Personal Information for the period necessary as long as your user account remains active, to enforce our agreements, resolve disputes, and unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

We may use any aggregated data derived from or incorporating your Personal Information after you update or delete it, but not in a manner that would identify you personally. Once the retention period expires, Personal Information shall be deleted. Therefore, the right to access, the right to erasure, the right to rectification, and the right to data portability cannot be enforced after the expiration of the retention period.

Geolocation Information

The app does not collect your location continuously or without your knowledge on any of the pages of the App.

The app does not use the Geolocation service and will not ask you for permission to use GPS.

Data Analytics

Our Mobile Application and Services use third-party analytics tools, Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, that use information-gathering technologies to collect standard internet activity and usage information.

The information gathered is used to compile statistical reports on user activity such as how often users visit our Mobile Application and Services and the app general usage trend.

We use the information obtained from these analytics tools to monitor the performance and improve our Mobile Application and Services. We do not use third-party analytics tools to track or to collect any personally identifiable information of our users and we will not associate any information gathered from the statistical reports with any individual user.

Push Notifications

We offer push notifications to which you may voluntarily subscribe at any time. To make sure push notifications reach the correct devices, we use a third-party push notifications provider, Firebase, who relies on a unique token for each device but it does not share or hold any personal information or identification. At any time you wish to stop receiving push notifications, simply adjust your device settings accordingly.

Links to Other Resources

The Mobile Application and Services does not contain any links to external sources.

Information Security

We secure information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards in an effort to protect against unauthorized access, use, modification, and disclosure of Personal Information in our control and custody.
